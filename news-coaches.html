A Grade Coaches Reappointed

Published: 11 Apr 2026

Club Updates By Blurr FC Editorial 11 Apr 2026 4 min read

Blurr Football Club has confirmed the reappointment of both A Grade coaches for the upcoming campaign, reinforcing a clear focus on continuity, performance, and long-term player development.

The Board highlighted strong cultural standards, improved team discipline, and measurable on-field growth over the previous season as key reasons behind the decision. This strategic move underscores management's confidence in the coaching group's vision and execution capabilities.

With preseason planning now finalized, the coaching group will prioritize match-tempo conditioning, midfield structure, and younger-player integration ahead of Round 1. The focus on integrated squad development ensures that talent progression remains a cornerstone of the club's competitive strategy moving forward.

Club leadership also noted that retaining experienced coaches provides stability across all squads and gives players a clear tactical direction entering a critical year. This continuity is expected to minimize transition disruption and maximize performance consistency across all grades.
